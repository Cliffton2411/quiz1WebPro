Local Foods Here


  • Sate Bebek

    • In other areas, duck is processed into a side dish or soup dish, in South Tangerang, duck is processed in a different way. Here, duck is served as an appetizing satay menu.How not, because the duck, which is famous for its slightly tough meat, is served in a special way. So it tastes more delicious and the meat is more tender.Duck satay is made by several processes. First, the duck meat is soaked before going through the roasting process. After that, the duck meat must be pierced using a special bamboo.If you have, the skewered duck meat is half cooked. Then, put again into the seasoning mixed with soy sauce and burned until cooked.

  • Sate Bandeng

    • Besides duck satay, there is also a special culinary that cannot be found in other areas, namely Milkfish Sate. This culinary is a popular food that is always hunted by tourists because of its distinctive and appetizing taste.How not, the culinary that is included in the list of 13 typical Tangerang foods is processed with spices and special cooking techniques.Before being processed into satay, the milkfish spines are removed first. Then, the milkfish is clamped using wooden or bamboo slats.In order for the spices to absorb, then put it in a bag of milkfish with the bamboo. After that, the milkfish satay is roasted until cooked.

  • Garang Asem

    • The next food that you must try is Garang Asem. This food that is suitable to be eaten when the weather is hot has a refreshing taste from the sauce because of the spicy taste and added sourness.Garang Asem is a favorite food in Tangerang because of its delicious taste that comes from the savory coconut milk and vegetable starfruit.The basic ingredients used to make one of the 13 culinary specialties of Tangerang are duck meat and chicken meat.Then, this meat will be cooked using banana leaves and doused with liquid coconut milk. But before that, the liquid coconut milk was given a mixture of spices. Then, the Garang Asem is steamed until cooked and ready to be served.
